Shazan Jabbar

Senior Applied Scientist + AI Tech Lead

I work on bringing ML and AI systems into production. Currently at Diligent Inc. in Vancouver, where I lead AI research for enterprise services deployed globally.

Previously, I built recommendation systems at GoDaddy and led applied ML projects at Amii (Alberta Machine Intelligence Institute), advising startups and Fortune 100 companies on their ML strategy.

I'm also the co-founder of BendAI, an LLM-powered software design agent that reasons over codebases and technical documents.

I received my M.Sc. in Computing Science from the University of Alberta (advised by Prof. Osmar Zaiane) and a B.Sc. (First Class Honours) from the University of Colombo.
